Animation

Character animation • Smooth timing • Motion tests • Expressive loops

I focus on clean, expressive character movement — from Roblox animation to motion tests, FX, and polished loops. My work aims to create animation with weight, emotion, and clarity, using smooth, readable timing. I specialize in bringing characters to life through intentional movement: reactions, turns, idles, and subtle emotional cues.

I create animation cycles, short-form character moments, expressive poses, and seamless transitions. My style leans toward emotion-driven animation, where personality comes through in the details — speed variation, anticipation, follow-through, squash and stretch, and controlled exaggeration. Every piece I create is designed to feel clean, satisfying, and loop-friendly.

This page serves as my dedicated space for showcasing animation clips, tests, experiments, and final reels — all presented within my refined Sebi-inspired site aesthetic.
